Gas Plant Tutorial

 

In this example we examine in detail the simulation of a simple natural gas plant. We have a natural gas feed that we want to process and produce a sales gas that has a specified dew point temperature for a given operating pressure. Please compare this example with the refrigeration plant example discussed in a previous section. The refrigeration plant was created using a more procedural style where unit operations were created, connected and finally automatically solved when the connections were completed. In this example we will proceed in a less structured manner to show the fully interactive nature of Sim42. Naturally you are free to choose your work style and Sim42 will always properly keep track of over specifications and connectivity.
