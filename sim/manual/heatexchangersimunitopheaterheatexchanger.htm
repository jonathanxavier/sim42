HeatExchanger - sim.unitop.Heater.HeatExchanger

 

The heat exchanger unit operation models a two-sided heat exchanger.

 

exch = Heater.HeatExchanger()

 

Item

Value

Sim42 File

 

Heater.py

 

Example

 

heatex.tst

 

InH Material Port

 

Heat exchanger hot side inlet

 

InC Material Port

 

Heat exchanger cold side inlet

 

OutH Material Port

 

Heat exchanger hot side outlet

 

Out CMaterial Port

 

Heat exchanger cold side outlet

 

DeltaPH

 

Pressure drop across hot side

 

DeltaPC

 

Pressure drop across cold side

 

DeltaTHI

 

Temperature approach at hot inlet

 

DeltaTHO

 

Temperature approach at hot outlet

 

IsCounterCurrent

 

Flag to signal flow type. Default is countercurrent (1). Parallel flow is flagged using (0)

 

Equations

 

 

Note that an error will be flagged if a temperature cross is detected.
