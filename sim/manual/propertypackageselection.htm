Property Package Selection

 

The proper selection of a property package is one of the most important aspects in creating a representative simulation and companies such as Virtual Materials Group can provide you with expert assistance on the selection, tuning and creation of thermodynamic models.

 

This section is specific to thermodynamic models provided by Virtual Materials Group. Please note that the suggestions herein are based on principles that are known to work well in industrial practice, but they are not a guarantee that they will always work.

Detailed model descriptions are available in the Thermodynamic Models manual available from Virtual Materials Group.

Seek expert advice whenever necessary.
