Create Nested Flowsheet for Dew Point Calculations

 

We want to specify the chiller operating conditions to provide us with the desired sales gas dew point specification. It is convenient for us to define a nested flowsheet to control the calculation sequence for us.

 

From Sim42's point of view a flowsheet is just another unit operation. You can create as many flow sheets as you would like as well as nest them in any way you find convenient.
