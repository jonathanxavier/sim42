Pump - sim.unitop.Pump.Pump

 

The pump unit operation performs the mechanical energy balance necessary to determine the power necessary to move a liquid fluid for a given pressure increase. Note that the pump performs an ideal energy balance and therefore it does not require pump curves.

 

p = Pump.Pump()
