PumpWithCurve - sim.unitop.Pump.PumpWithCurve

 

The PumpWithCurve unit operation performs the mechanical energy balance necessary to determine the power necessary to move a liquid fluid for a given pressure increase taking into account the pump characteristics as represented by the pump curves.

 

p = Pump.PumpWithCurve()
