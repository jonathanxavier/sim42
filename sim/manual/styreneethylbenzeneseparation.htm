Styrene / Ethylbenzene Separation

 

After styrene is produced it has to be separated from Ethyl Benzene and the Ethyl Benzene is recycled. Since styrene polymerizes at high temperatures the distillation has to be performed at vacuum.

This apparently simple example is a good test to ensure the accuracy of the property package since it is being used to calculate vapor pressures of close boiling compounds. Several commercial simulators have failed in the simulation of this system as shown by Sadeq et al. (1997)

Sim42’s results compare well against the standard solution to this problem as published by Holland (1981). All flow rates are in kmol/h
